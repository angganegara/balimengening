Bali Mengening Villa

A Private beach front villa at BALI - INDONESIA

Welcome to bali mengening villa , an absolute beach front villa at mengening bali

Our villas are named bali mengening villa after the peaceful beach they are located, the place where people get away from the crowds to feel tranquility.

The villas are situated on the curve beach line forming an arch give a breathtaking panorama view of indian ocean and surrounded by vast rice field.

People come here to enjoy the best sunset view of bali, place for many marriage couple to take their prewedding picture background.

The villas are also located in the traditional balinese village cemagi, one of the major rice producer to its region.


The Villa is located on Cemagi village part of South West Bali area Cemagi village is about a :

- 20 minutes drive to the Canggu Club

- 25 minutes drive to the heart of Seminyak

- 45 minutes drive from the International Airport

- 10 minutes drive to the Nirwana Bali Golf Club.
